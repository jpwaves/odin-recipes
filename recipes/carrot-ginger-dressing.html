Carrot Ginger Dressing

Description

Bored of plain salads? Revamp your salad experience with this sweet, tangy, zesty dressing! Not only is it a healthy alternative to store-bought salad dressings, but it also elevates the taste of simple salads!

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot
  • 1/4 onion
  • 1 knob ginger
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp miso
  • 1/4 tsp kosher/sea salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (roasted)
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup netural-flavored oil (vegetable, rice bran, canola, etc.)

Steps

  1. Measure all the ingredients.
  2. Pell the carrot and chop into 1/2 to 1 inch pieces.
  3. Chop onion into 1 inch pieces.
  4. Peel ginger and slice into small pieces. Put all chopped vegetables into the food processor.
  5. Puree the vegetables until fine and smooth.
  6. Add sugar, miso, salt, and pepper.
  7. Add sesame oil, rice vinegar, and neutral-flavored oil.
  8. Process until the dressing is completely smooth. Taste test dressing and adjust with salt to taste. If it's too sour, add more sugar. Add water if you want to thin out dressing.
  9. Shake well before serving!
